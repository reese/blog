## Acknowledgements

I know I said I made a thing, but really, I only made a small contribution on top of several amazing pieces of prior art.
First and foremost, the conceit of the post came from Kyle Kingsbury's "---ing the Technical Interview" series, especially ["Rewriting the Technical Interview"](https://aphyr.com/posts/353-rewriting-the-technical-interview).
I knew I wanted to pay homage to the series _somehow_, and doing gross things with web technologies seemed like a great start.

As for the weird live-coding presentation of the story, a lot of that is inspired by Samuel Reed's [personal site](https://www.strml.net/), which is in turn inspired by the work of [Jake Albaugh](http://codepen.io/jakealbaugh/).
